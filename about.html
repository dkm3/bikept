What is a Bicycle Friendly Community?

Provincetown uses the 5-E's, a framework for defining a bicycle friendly community as set forth by:
The League for American Cyclists

Engineering

Providing safe and convenient places to ride and park a bicycle

Education

Giving people of all ages and abilities the skills and confidence to ride

Enforcement

Ensuring roads are safe for all users: bicyclists, pedestrians and vehicles

Encouragement

Creating a strong bike culture that welcomes and celebrates bicycling

Evaluation

Planning for bicycling as a safe and viable transportation option
