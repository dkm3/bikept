The Cape Cod National Seashore

Province Lands Bicycle Network

This asphalt multiuse path is a 5.45 mile loop through the national seashore with spurs for access to Herring Cove, Race Point Beach and Bennett Pond.

Length:

5.45 Miles.

Max-Speed:

10 MPH

Access:

Enter from Beech Forest, Visitors Center or at Pilgrim's Landing Park.

Difficulty:

Advanced-Intermediate

Hazards:

steep hills, sharp turns, tunnels, sand, potholes, animal crossing, active roadway intersections, no artificial lighting
